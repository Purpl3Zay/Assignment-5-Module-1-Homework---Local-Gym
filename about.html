Crossfit

 Our crossfit class is designed for beginners and the more experiances alike. We have different classes for different levels as well as intensities for our older community who is trying to stay in shape.

Yoga

 We offer a guided yoga class complete with sanitized mats, a licensed instructor, guided meditation, and soft scents of calming insence wafting through the class. We offer in person classes as well as guided yoga zoom classes where you can stay healthy in the comfort of your own home.

Cycling

 We offer an array of cycling classes from a short bike down the street with calming music in the background to intense Tour de France levels courses with bumping music to get you pumped. Whether youre Lance Armstrong or your only experience is Wii Sports Resort we have something for you.

Basketball

 In our open gym we have two seperate courts, one designated for a shoot around with ball returns under the nets to warm up and another that is set aside to run pick up games. We also offer events and tournements for charity and with cash prizes for the winners.
