Who We Are

The Mocha Dojo is a unique gym/cafe that offers a variety of activities for its members. This includes, but is not limited to crossfit, yoga, cycling, free gym basketball, as well as a cafe filled with healthy beverages and snacks. The staff at The Mocha Dojo are dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for people of all ages and skill levels to enjoy their time, while achieving their goals, all while promoting body positivity.

After a workout, guests can enjoy refreshing and nourishing beverages like coffees and milkshakes at The Mocha Dojo's fully-equipped cafe. Visitors can also take part in a variety of recreational activities in the gym's multi-purpose space, including yoga, karate, and even rock climbing. With a strong foundation in mindfulness and self-care, The Mocha Dojo is designed to serve as a sanctuary for guests seeking a peaceful break from their busy and stressful daily lives. The atmosphere of the facilities invites everyone - from adults to children - to explore and enjoy the many beneficial effects of physical and mental wellness.
